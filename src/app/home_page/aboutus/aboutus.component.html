About Us

Think Ahoy brings entrepreneurs, mentors, investors and business service providers onto one single platform.

Think Ahoy aims to democratise the concept of building companies by breaking down barriers such as geographical differences and helps establish effective business partnerships to solve problems. Think Ahoy is one platform that bridges the divide between mentors, investors, business service providers and entrepreneurs across the globe. By Think-ing Ahoy, one can establish and nurture businesses while learning from the best to solve real problems and craft fascinating stories.
